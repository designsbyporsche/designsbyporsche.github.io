About Porsche

Meet Your Designer

I am Portia, originally from Katlehong, with a professional background in the finance sector, where I specialised in credit and collections. While I have always valued detail and excellent customer service, my true passion lies in creating beautiful, warm homes.

My journey into interior design began after a disappointing renovation experience. I realised there was a need for change in how homeowners are treated and in the quality of service they receive. That's why I founded Designs by Porsche — to bring homeowners not only classy, timeless designs but also the exceptional service they truly deserve.
